FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Once you book a test, our certified phlebotomist will visit your home at the scheduled time. They follow strict safety protocols, collect the sample, and transport it to the lab in temperature-controlled containers.

We work with India's top NABL-accredited labs including Thyrocare, Redcliffe Labs, and Healthians. All labs follow stringent quality standards.

Most reports are delivered within 6-24 hours of sample collection. You'll receive them via WhatsApp and email. Some specialized tests may take 2-3 days.

Fasting of 10-12 hours is required for lipid profile, blood sugar (fasting), and full body checkups. We recommend booking an early morning slot for fasting tests.

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, and cash on collection. Payment can be made at the time of booking or after sample collection.
